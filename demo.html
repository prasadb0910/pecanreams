About Pecan Reams

We strongly believed in the need for digital solutions to simplify real estate asset management which led to the birth of Pecan Group .

Under the umbrella of our real estate solutions we provide the below products,

  • Pecan Reams -Real estate asset and portfolio management platform.
  • iDATA - Access to in-depth knowledge & market insights about the Real estate.
  • Assure - Keeps our clients abreast about public notices concerning their Immovable properties.
  • Pecan Advisors - Supporting our clients in the process of Fund raising , Mergers & Acquisitions and Joint ventures.

Mission

Use Technology and on the ground support to solve day to day issues faced in Real Estate Asset Management and there by maximise the property value and investment returns.

Our Values

Honesty & Integrity

These are the foremost values for us in life. These value systems are what define each of us as individuals and Pecan as a firm.




Partnership Approach

We conduct business with our clients in the spirit of partnership, thriving to achieve the best for them. By this, our focus is client objectives and needs and we do everything possible to achieve those.


Build Trust

We believe that in the long run we will be successful if we have been able to build and retain trust. Our approach to relationships is building them with trust with clients, market players, team members, shareholders, investors & society at large.

Stewardship

We believe in acumen stewardship and hence whatever projects or activities we have taken up, we ensure to leave them better than before. Through constant efforts we strive hard to ensure that the best possible.

Rohit Garodia

Founder & Managing Director

Rohit Garodia is the founder of the Pecan group which spans Investment Banking, Retail, Real Estate, Portfolio Management and Angel investing. He has more than a decade of combined experience in real estate investments, commercial and investment banking, and early stage start- up companies. Rohit had conceptualized Pecan REAMS given the rising need of people for a digital solution to help manage their Real Estate Assets. Prior to starting Pecan, he was Director and Co-Head – Real Estate for JM Financial, one of India’s largest financial services player. Responsible for the Real Estate business including investing, lending & advisory services and served on board of portfolio companies. He has detailed knowledge of all major real estate markets in India and of real estate rules & regulations. Rohit is an Associate Chartered Accountant from Institute of Chartered Accountants of India where he was ranked among the top 1% of his graduating class and he is did his undergrad from Narsee Monjee College, Mumbai.

Kumar Khatri
