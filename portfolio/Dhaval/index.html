Dhaval Maru

Technology Head

Dhaval Maru is responsible for managing the entire IT architecture for Pecan Reams. He pursued a degree in BE in Information Technology and owned a technology setup with a team of ten IT Professionals before being a part of the company. At the initial stage of his career he started with his own commercial product walking media i.e. T-shirt with TV which gained him a huge recognition and reward, with the coverage in newspapers like Times of India, Mid-day, Sandesh. He also won Graham Bell award from the Aegies School of Business for the same product. He has also been invited to take lectures in Shah and Anchor Kutchhi Polytechnic by Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education. His areas of research and teaching are System Architecture and Database Design, Software Development, Human Computer Interaction, Web Development, Ecommerce Application Development and Big Data Management. He possess an excellent eye to details with good logical, analytical and mathematical skills coupled with patience and perseverance.
