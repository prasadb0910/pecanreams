Rohit Garodia

Founder & Managing Director

Head – Real Estate & Alternative Asset Management

Rohit Garodia is the founder and Managing Director of Pecan Group and he has over 15 years of combined work-experience. Capitalizing on his vast real estate knowledge and experience, strong financial and entrepreneurial acumen he has lead Pecan Group to establish itself as a trusted organisation in the field of Real Estate Tech-Services, E- Commerce and Healthy Snacks industry. Since 2010 Pecan Group has grown to a large family of 100+ personnel

Prior to finding Pecan Group he was the Director and Co-Head – Real Estate for JM Financial, one of India’s largest financial services institutions globally where he was responsible for originating and executing deals across a range of cities and asset classes for the JM Financial Property Fund and served on the board of its portfolio companies. Prior to this he was in the Investment Banking group at JM Morgan Stanley in the Corporate Finance coverage group. He worked on the demerger of Reliance Industries Limited and on the first entity- level FDI in real estate in India. Prior to that, Rohit worked with ICICI Bank in their Corporate Banking Group and founded a computer hardware start- up.
