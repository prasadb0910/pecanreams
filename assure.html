About Assure

Any user finds it tedious to track all 42 newspapers on a daily basis. Assure steps in and notifies the user regarding any public notice issued on his equity or real estate assets. Assure is our online depository of Public Notices. We capture public notices from over 42 national and local newspapers in Mumbai in English, Marathi and Hindi languages. Our system helps user to access public notices published on his Assets helping them in making well-informed decisions.

Developers, Lawyers, Financial Institutions and Investors helping them with title checks, identifying issues related to assets registered and taking well informed decision.
